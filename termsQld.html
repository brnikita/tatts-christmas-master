    1. The Game is played as follows:
      1. There are 16 covered panels in the Game; and
      2. The Entrant will: 
        1. choose any 2 of the 16 covered panels; and
        2. digitally ‘scratch’ the 2 chosen panels to reveal a pictorial symbol in each panel; and
      3. If the 2 pictorial symbols revealed in the Entrants chosen 2 panels are not exactly the same, the Entrant may continue to play until they have completed the number of turns applicable to the Tier they have entered (eg. 3 turns for Tier 1, 5 turns for Tier 2 and 5 turns for Tier 3); or

    2. An Eligible Winner will be notified immediately on the online screen with a message “Congratulations! You have matched 2 symbols” if the 2 pictorial symbols in their chosen 2 panels are exactly the same.

      1. click the “Claim Prize” button on the online screen; and
      2. complete the online prize claim form within 24 hours from when they are notified that they are an Eligible Winner in clauses  8.1(d) and 8.2; and
      3. submit the completed online prize claim form by clicking the “CLAIM NOW” button on the online screen.

      2. If the Eligible Winner does complete the prize claim verification in accordance with the terms and conditions relevant to that Tier they will become a winner of a prize in that Tier (Winning Entrant).
